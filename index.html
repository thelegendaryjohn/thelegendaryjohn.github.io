About


I'm Johnni-P, usually known as Johnny, a decently normal guy who likes to cover songs in his free time using the power of voice synthesizing technology because I can't sing... ;-;

My goal is to recreate how an original song sounds as closely as possible to my best effort. I sometimes work on other projects as well, like making remix and stuff, related to vocal synth or not
